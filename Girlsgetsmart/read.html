Nobody can give you wiser advice than yourself   ~Marcus Tullius Cicero (106-43 BC)

This in not a book of advice; it is a book of stories. We hope this is a book that will inspire and guide you when you need it. This is a book to go to when you are too afraid to ask the question out loud or have no one to turn to.

  • Why this book?
  • Why listen to us?
  • How do we Know?

Well, we are all women~which means we were once girls, too. We have daughters. We are also all doctors who have helped many, many teenage girls through some very difficult times. We have tried to guide them in making the right decisions, and we have helped them overcome or heal when they have made the wrong ones.

We want to help you make the right decisions so that you can survive this time called adolescence and grow to become the woman you were meant to be. We believe that for each young women we help become a success, we have also touched the lives of everyone she will touch.
