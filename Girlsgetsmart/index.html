Get Smart A Teenage Girl's Ultimate Survival Guide arms teen girls with the tools they need, offering up facts and real-life stories from the authors' own experiences with practicing medicine.

After years spent helping countless girls get through puberty and adolescence, doctors Susan Mehnert-Kay, Kristina Kline, and Gwendolyn Gibson have pretty much seen it all. Tired of treating girls woefully unprepared for their teenage years, the authors teamed up to write a frank, eye-opening account of the various experiences-both potential and guaranteed-that the young girl undergoes as she journeys toward becoming a young woman.

Covered in this must-have book are:

  • Dating
  • The advent of puberty and menustration
  • Mental health, including healthy body image
  • And much, much more...

Don't make the journey alone-arm yourself with the knowledge you need to get smart and survive those teenage year!
